Welcome

Assa’s Shop Limited has proudly served the Chestergreen and Derby community for over 30 years. Founded by Mrs Assa Basi and now led by her daughter Enisha Basi Kainth, the shop continues to be built on kindness, support and service.

What began as a local grocery store has grown into a trusted community hub — supporting families, young people, elders and those in need with compassion, dignity and care.

Today, Assa’s Shop Limited is not just a place to buy essentials — it is a place of connection, encouragement and community leadership.

Honouring the legacy of Mrs Assa Basi and the family leadership behind Assa’s Shop Limited.
